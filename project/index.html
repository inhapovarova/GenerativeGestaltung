Generative exhibition website

the city that remebers

Explore a small city archive made from p5.js generators, saved facades, social media compositions, and printable exhibition assets.

What you can try

Build a single house, generate a moving street, compose a poster, create an Instagram-style post, or design an exhibition ticket. Save your favourites and collect them in the gallery.

How it works

1. Generate a city image on one of the generator pages.
2. Use the save button to store the PNG through the local server.
3. Open the gallery to see saved buildings, streets, and visual experiments.
